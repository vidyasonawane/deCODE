Ramesh: the programmer

You give me a coding problem, I can read and understand it, I also know the basics of syntax. But when it comes to solving the question on my own, I get stuck. Though I know the theory behind the problem, I don’t know how to start solving it from scratch.

If you think, you are a Ramesh - you are at the right place!
You can’t deny that logic is the fundamental key to become a good developer. If you don’t understand how to turn your thoughts into code, this course is for you!
